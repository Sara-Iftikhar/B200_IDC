# Infectious Disease Bioinformatics Course @ KAUST

This is a 10-day intensive course structured over two weeks:

**Week 1:** Online lectures covering epidemiology and infectious diseases, including a guest lecture by **Dr. Abrar Alasmari** from the **Ministry of Health (MOH)** on **18 June 2026**.

**Week 2:** In-person practical sessions focused on genomics, phylogenetics, and outbreak analysis.

The course blends theoretical learning with hands-on analysis using **R, Linux, and HPC systems**.

**Dates:** 14–25 June 2026  
**Format:** Week 1 online lectures + Week 2 in-person practicals
